Korean Chicken

Description

Fried chicken in a spicy, korean, red bean paste sauce

Ingredients

Seasoning

  • 2 Chicken Breast
  • 3 tsp Paprika
  • 3 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Sauce

  • 3 tbp Water
  • 3 tbp Light Soy Sauce
  • 25g Honey
  • 40g Gochujang Paste

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes and mix with the seasoning until the colour changes
  2. Add the seasoned chicken to a frying pan and cook on medium temp for 8 minutes or until 73 degrees C
  3. Put the chicken aside, and in the same frying pan add the water soy sauce honey and gochujang paste on low temp
  4. Stir the sauce until thick
  5. Add the chicken back into the frying pan and cook for another minute
  6. Serve the chicken with rice
