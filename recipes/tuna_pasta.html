Tuna Mayo Pasta

Description

Fusili pasta mixed with tuna, sweetcorn and drowned in mayonaise

Ingredients

  • 1 Bowl Fusili Pasta
  • 2 Tins Tuna in Spring Water
  • 1 Tin Sweetcorn
  • 3 Dollops Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Boil a kettle and pour some water and salt into the saucepan and place over a hot stove
  2. Put the pasta into the pot and pour in the boiling water from the kettle, then place the lid on the saucepan
  3. Cook the pasta in the boiling water for 15 minutes, making sure not to let the water boil over the pan
  4. Once cooked, empty the water from the saucepan and replace with cold water to cool the pasta
  5. Empty the cold water from the pan and then add the rest of the ingredients, then stir
